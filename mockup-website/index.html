Our story

A Thai home in the middle of London

Baan Siam means "Thai home" — and that is exactly what we set out to build. A quiet, family-run sanctuary in Fitzrovia where traditional Thai massage is done properly: 60 minutes hands-on, no upsells, no rushing.

Founded by Khun Anong Chai in 2018 after fifteen years training in Chiang Mai's traditional schools. Every therapist here was personally selected by Anong and has at least eight years of practice.
