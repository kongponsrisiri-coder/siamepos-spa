Our story

A second home for Thai healing in London

Baan Siam was founded by Khun Anong Chai in 2018 after fifteen years training in Chiang Mai's traditional schools. The name means simply "Thai home" — and that's what we set out to build.

Every therapist here has at least eight years of practice. We work in soft light, with herbal compresses we steam ourselves, and we never rush — a 60-minute treatment really is 60 minutes on the table.
