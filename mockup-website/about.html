Our story

A Thai home in Fitzrovia

Family-trained therapists. Traditional methods. A quiet room three minutes from Goodge Street.

Founded 2018

Why we opened Baan Siam

Baan Siam means "Thai home" — and that is exactly what we wanted to build. A quiet, family-run sanctuary where Thai massage is done the way it is done in Chiang Mai: slowly, deliberately, with proper time on the table.

Our founder, Khun Anong Chai, trained for over a decade at the traditional schools in northern Thailand before bringing the practice to London. Every therapist here was personally selected by Anong and has at least eight years of experience.

We work in soft light, with herbal compresses we steam ourselves each morning, and we never overbook — you get the full session you have paid for. A pandan tea ritual closes every treatment so you have time to come back to yourself before stepping back out onto Charlotte Street.

The Baan Siam difference

Authentic technique
