Our story

A Thai home in Fitzrovia

Baan Siam means "Thai home" — and that's exactly what we wanted to build. A quiet, family-run sanctuary where Thai massage is done the way it's done in Chiang Mai: slowly, deliberately, with proper time on the table.

Our founder, Khun Anong Chai, trained for over a decade at the traditional schools in Chiang Mai before bringing the practice to London in 2018. Every therapist here was personally selected by Anong and has at least eight years of experience.

We work in soft light, with herbal compresses we steam ourselves each morning, and we never overbook — you get the full hour you've paid for. A tea ritual closes every treatment so you have time to come back to yourself before stepping out into Charlotte Street.

What sets us apart

The Baan Siam difference

🌿

Authentic technique

Every therapist trained in Thailand for a minimum of eight years before joining the team. We follow the Wat Pho lineage, not a generic spa-school certificate.

🕯

Time on the table

A 60-minute treatment really is 60 minutes hands-on. We don't bake in changing time, intake forms or upsells — that all sits around the appointment, not inside it.

🍵

The tea ritual

Every session closes with a quiet cup of pandan or ginger tea. Five minutes to land before you step back into London — included in every booking.
